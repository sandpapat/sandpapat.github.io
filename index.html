Bio

Part-time introvert, full-time overthinker who somehow ended up teaching machines to teach humans English grammar (yes, the irony isn't lost on me). When I'm not debugging why my AI thinks 'I will had been going' is a valid tense, you'll find me at the go-kart track pretending I'm Alex Albon (fellow Thai representing!), where friends constantly roast me for being way too competitive in rental karts and spending an embarrassing amount on racing gloves and shoes for someone who tops out at 40mph. I oscillate between wanting to discuss the philosophical implications of neural networks at parties and hiding in the corner with my headphones on. My Spotify wrapped is a chaotic mix of lofi study beats, eurobeat racing soundtracks, and whatever's playing at Silverstone that weekend. Currently training AI to be a better language teacher than me, which is either brilliant or career suicide – jury's still out. I treat every code deployment like a pit stop, watch football with the same intensity as qualifying sessions, and yes, I've definitely explained grammar tenses using racing lines as an analogy. Will argue that go-karting is the purest form of motorsport while simultaneously justifying why I 'need' a proper helmet for indoor karting. Send help (or better GPU resources... or sponsor my karting dreams).
